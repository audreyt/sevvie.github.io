The wrong kind of smart

Of recent, some wisdom was imparted to me that, while somewhat jarring and upsetting, was exactly what I needed to hear. They're perhaps the most intelligent person I know, and they let me know that, if we were standing together at the edge of a cliff with a length of rope, and I had the idea to go rappelling, the first action he would take would be to assure that I had secured the rope -- forward thinking isn't always my first virtue, when I am excited by the merit of an idea, for better or for worse.
