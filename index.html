The Road to Hell is Paved with the Best of Intentions.

I had intended to write a post about my endeavours, today, from my new home, temporary or otherwise, and how those endeavours landed me in such an affordable and accomodating place; unfortunately, as of yet, there's just nothing to write. My plans, backup plans, and contingencies have all fallen through, and I'm in a state of free-fall. I'm only writing to keep my wits about me; if there's one thing life has taught me, it's that losing one's head is the quickest way to make a tough situation into a bad situation, and a bad situation into a terrible situation. For now, I'll take tough.
